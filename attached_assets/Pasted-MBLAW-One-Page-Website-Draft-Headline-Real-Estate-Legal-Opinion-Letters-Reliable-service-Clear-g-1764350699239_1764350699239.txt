Who We Are

MBLAW is a boutique legal practice focused exclusively on delivering real estate legal opinion letters for commercial loan transactions. With extensive experience across complex financing structures, MBLAW provides borrowers and lenders with accurate, timely, and meticulously prepared opinion letters that support smooth closings and reduce friction in the deal process.

We prioritize clarity, responsiveness, and professional precision—ensabling clients to proceed with confidence at every stage of their transaction.

⸻

Opinion Services

MBLAW prepares a range of commonly required real estate opinion letters, including:
	•	Enforceability Opinions
	•	Entity & Authority Opinions
	•	Single-Purpose Entity (SPE) / Non-Dissolution Opinions
	•	Substantive Non-Consolidation Opinions
	•	UCC-Related Opinions
	•	Additional opinion matters upon request

All opinions are delivered with a focus on consistency, accuracy, and closing-ready language tailored to the needs of each deal.

Why Clients Choose MBLAW

Responsive Service

Transactions move quickly, and so do we. MBLAW is committed to delivering prompt communication, efficient coordination with deal counsel, and reliable turnaround times to help ensure smooth closings.

Competitive, Predictable Fees

As a specialized opinion practice, we offer flat-fee opinion services with transparent pricing. Clients can request a quote at the outset of a deal, allowing them to plan ahead and avoid unexpected costs.

Focused Expertise

Our practice is dedicated solely to real estate legal opinion work. This narrow focus allows us to provide precise, well-crafted opinions that meet lender requirements and industry standards.

Seamless Collaboration

We work directly with borrower’s counsel, lender’s counsel, and deal teams to finalize opinion requirements, helping streamline the closing process and reduce unnecessary delays.
